Quick Generation Tools

Use these online tools to generate all favicon sizes from a single image:

  • RealFaviconGenerator.net - Recommended (most comprehensive)
  • Favicon.io - Simple and fast
  • Favicon Generator - Alternative option

Steps to Generate

  1. Prepare your source image (PNG, JPG, or SVG) - should be at least 512x512 pixels
  2. Visit RealFaviconGenerator
  3. Upload your source image
  4. Configure settings:
    • Enable iOS (Apple touch icon) - 180x180
    • Enable Android Chrome - 192x192 and 512x512
    • Enable Desktop favicons - 16x16 and 32x32
  5. Click "Generate your Favicons and HTML code"
  6. Download the generated package
  7. Extract and copy all PNG/ICO files to the public/ folder
  8. Replace existing files if prompted
