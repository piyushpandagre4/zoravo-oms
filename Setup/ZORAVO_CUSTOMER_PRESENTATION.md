## üìä Complete Business Workflow

### End-to-End Customer Journey

#### **Step 1: Vehicle Intake**
- Customer arrives with vehicle
- Staff registers vehicle in system with:
  - Vehicle details (registration, make, model, year, color)
  - Customer information (name, phone, email, address)
  - Service requirements
  - Estimated cost and duration
- System automatically creates vehicle record
- Status set to "Pending"

#### **Step 2: Service Planning**
- Manager/Coordinator reviews vehicle intake
- Creates service tracker entry
- Assigns installer based on workload
- Sets priority level
- System sends notification to assigned installer

#### **Step 3: Work Execution**
- Installer receives work order
- Updates status as work progresses:
  - In Progress
  - Under Installation
  - Installation Complete
- Adds notes and updates
- System tracks time and progress

#### **Step 4: Quality Check & Completion**
- Manager reviews completed work
- Updates status to "Completed"
- System generates invoice automatically
- Customer notified via email/WhatsApp

#### **Step 5: Delivery & Payment**
- Customer arrives for pickup
- Payment recorded in system
- Status updated to "Delivered"
- Invoice marked as paid
- System updates customer history

#### **Step 6: Follow-up & Retention**
- Automated follow-up call scheduled
- Customer satisfaction tracked
- Service history maintained
- Repeat customer identification
